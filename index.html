about us

Purpose:

This platform is to connect Kenyan farmers to fair markets, essential resources, giving them mentors, and also ensuring their harvest thrive and their efforts are rewarded.

Origin story:

Seeing my father's hard-grown produce spoil in our farm due to lack of buyers, I was inspired to build the platform of Agro Connect. I know that he is not the only farmer who faces this problem.
